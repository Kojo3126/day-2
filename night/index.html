Gary is a sea snail with a pink shell which depicts purplish-dark blue spots and a red spiral. He has eye antennas that hold together his scarlet-green eyes with orangish-red pupils. The sides of his body are light blue and his belly is green.

He is typically known to create trails of slime when crawling.
